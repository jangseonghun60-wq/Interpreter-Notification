Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Interpreter Notification Zoom App ("the App") handles information.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No user information is retained after the meeting ends.

2. Data Usage

The App uses the Zoom Apps SDK solely to send in-meeting notification messages from the interpreter to meeting participants. No data is shared with third parties.

3. Cookies

The App does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

4. User Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. Users may contact us at sh.jang@krafton.com with any privacy-related questions.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

6. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact: sh.jang@krafton.com
