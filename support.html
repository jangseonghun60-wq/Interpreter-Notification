Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use this app?

This app is designed for interpreters at Krafton. Only the interpreter needs to install and open the app during a meeting.

Do participants need to install anything?

No. Participants do not need to install anything. They will receive notifications automatically when the interpreter sends a message.

What messages can the interpreter send?

The interpreter can send pre-set notification messages such as "Please speak closer to the mic", "Please speak slower", and "Audio is unclear".

How long do notifications appear?

Notifications appear for 5 seconds and then disappear automatically.
